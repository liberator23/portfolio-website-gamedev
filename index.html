An Introduction

Welcome to my website!

I'm a dual Canadian-German citizen, avid snowboarder, airsoft player, and game designer trying to push the boundaries of interactive entertainment. I'm currently earning my bachelors degree in game design at Hanze UAS in Groningen. You can find regular updates on my current work listed below.

My Story

Early Life - 2000

As a child I was exposed to video games on the Nintendo 64 from my sisters playing games like Mario Party and GoldenEye 007.

First Career - 2013

I became an Electricican and pursued an apprenticeship for four and a half years becoming a Red Seal Endorsed Journeyman Electrician.

Reflection - 2018

I began to think about what I wanted to do going forward into my life, I realized that I loved games and the experiences they convey.

Current Life - 2019

I made the decision to move across the globe to Groningen and begin my education in game design, I hope to enter the industry soon.
