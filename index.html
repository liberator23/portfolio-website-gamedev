I'm Justin Krogman

Game and Level Designer

Welcome

I'm a dual Canadian-German citizen, avid snowboarder, airsoft player, and game designer trying to push the boundaries of interactive entertainment. I'm currently pursuing an education in Game Design at Hanze University of Applied Sciences, with a primary goal of specializing in level design, open world design, and gameplay design. You can find regular updates on my current work listed below and through my social media channels.

My Story

Early Life 1995-2011

As a child I was exposed to video games on the Nintendo 64 from my sisters playing games like Mario Party and GoldenEye 007. I continued to enjoy video games from many genres and developed a strong passion for the medium.


First Career 2012-2017

I became a commerical Electricican and pursued an apprenticeship for four and a half years, eventually becoming a fully licensed and endorsed Red Seal Journeyman Electrician.

Reflection 2018

I began to think about what I really wanted to do going forward into my life, I realized that I loved games and the experiences they convey.

Changes 2019-2021

I made the decision to move across the globe to Groningen and begin my education in game design at Hanze Uniersiy of Applied Sciences, inspired and motivated to pursue my dream to become a game designer.

Current Life 2022 and Beyond

I have successfully completed the first three years of my bachelors degree in Communication and Multimedia Design at Hanze UAS, with a major in Game Design and a minor in Medialism. I have also successfully completed an internship at Dutch publishing studio Indietopia, giving me valuable indusry experience and preparing me for the final year of my degree program. I plan to enter the industry as a graduate student in summer of 2023.
